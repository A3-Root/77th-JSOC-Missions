[align=center][color=#FFD700][size=150][font=Arial][u]Situation[/u][/font][/size][/color][/align]


[align=justify] 
The 77th Joint Special Operations Command (JSOC) is currently deployed within Mexico as part of a high-priority mission aimed at countering the Sonora Cartel, which has been designated as a Terrorist Organization by the President of the United States. Our mission objectives are to initiate a decisive assault against the cartel, targeting its leadership, disrupting its operations, and unveiling critical information regarding its supply chain, strategic capabilities, and financial networks. The success of this mission will serve as a visible demonstration of our nation's commitment to combating this threat, with the aim of reassuring the American people.

[align=center][color=#FFD700][size=150][font=Arial][u]Objectives and Execution[/u][/font][/size][/color][/align]
 

[align=left] 
[b]1. Helicopter Insertion[/b]
Deltas will be inserted into the AO via UH-60M Blackhawk (EWS). It is imperative that we avoid the No-Fly zone.

[b]2. Capture/Kill "Gatekeeper"[/b]
- Our mission includes the capture or elimination of HVT "Gatekeeper," a pivotal figure within the cartel's leadership.
- If killed, bring proof of body back to base.

[b]3. Capture "Borat"[/b]
- Apprehend "Borat," a high-ranking member of the cartel, to interrogate, study, and disrupt their operations.

[b]4. Secure Mexican FOBs [/b]
- Assault and secure two FOBs.
- Rescue and Assist any Federale found.

[b]5. Secure Mexican CID[/b]
- Assault and secure the CID Base.
- Seize all available intelligence found.

[b]6. Disrupt Cartel Smuggling Post[/b]
- Assault Cartel's private airstrip.
- Neutralize all enemy forces in the area.
- Burn / Destroy all cartel products in the area.

[b]7. Investigate Cartel Activity[/b]
- Collect any intelligence materials found during the operation and act on the information.

[b]8. Rescue Mexican Federales[/b]
A critical humanitarian aspect of our mission involves rescuing Mexican Federal Police officers who may have been held captive by the cartel. [/align]
